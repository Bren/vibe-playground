Pixel Options

Interactive Pixel Grid

Click on pixels to toggle them on/off. Create your own pixel art!

How to use:

  • Click pixels to toggle them on/off
  • Clear All - Turn off all pixels
  • Randomize - Randomly turn on/off pixels
  • Invert - Flip all pixels (on becomes off, off becomes on)
  • Export Pattern - Copy the pattern as text
